Personal Injuries or Loss

On top of criminal proceedings injured workers can make civil claims for personal injury or loss.

Damages are awarded based on a range of things like the loss of sight or hearing, how long an injury lasts and how it affects the person's ability to earn a living.

Employer’s Liability Insurance

This insures employers for injury or disease sustained by employees in the course of their employment. The minimum level of insurance cover is £5 million.

Further Implications

The media attention on a serious accident will change the public's perception of your organisation. Loss of reputation is dreadful for business.

Costs to your Organisation

If the worst happens you need to be mindful of the extent of the costs to your organisation - think about the cost of replacement labour, accident investigation, downtime, and hikes in insurance premiums.
